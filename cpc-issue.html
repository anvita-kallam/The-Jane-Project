One critical goal of The Jane Project is to focus on combatting the deceptive reproductive services provided by anti-abortion Crisis Pregnancy Centers (CPCs). Crisis Pregnancy Centers are predatory, legal clinics that offer free but incomplete reproductive health services to vulnerable individuals. Their primary purpose is to prevent or deter a person from seeking selected reproductive healthcare options, including abortions. CPCs, which proliferate in Chester County, seek to intercept people who may not understand the predatory nature of the services CPCs provide, and through an information campaign, the JP will strive to create a barrier.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to the Jane Project?

We welcome contributions in many forms: code contributions, data submissions, content analysis, and community outreach. Visit our GitHub repository or contact us to learn more about specific opportunities.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share personal data with third parties without explicit consent.

How accurate is the fact-checking system?

Our AI-powered fact-checking system achieves 95%+ accuracy through continuous training and validation. However, we always recommend cross-referencing with multiple sources.

Can I submit data about a new center?

Absolutely! We encourage community submissions. Use our "Add Center" form or contact us directly. All submissions are reviewed for accuracy before being added to our database.

How often is the data updated?

Our database is updated continuously as new information becomes available. We conduct comprehensive reviews quarterly and welcome corrections from the community.

Do you offer training or workshops?

Yes! We offer workshops on data literacy, fact-checking techniques, and advocacy strategies. Contact us to schedule a session for your organization or community group.

Emergency Contact

For urgent matters requiring immediate attention, please use our emergency contact form or call our 24/7 hotline at 1-800-JANE-HELP.

Note: This hotline is for urgent advocacy and support matters only. For medical emergencies, please call 911 or contact your local emergency services.

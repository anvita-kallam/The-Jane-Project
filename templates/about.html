About Jane Project

Empowering communities with accurate information, advanced technology, and data-driven insights to combat misinformation in reproductive health advocacy.

Our Mission

The Jane Project is dedicated to providing accurate, evidence-based information about reproductive health while combating misinformation through technology, data visualization, and artificial intelligence. We believe in empowering communities with knowledge and tools to make informed decisions.

Our platform serves as a comprehensive resource for advocates, researchers, and community members seeking reliable information about Crisis Pregnancy Centers and reproductive health services.

Our Vision

We envision a world where accurate, evidence-based information about reproductive health is easily accessible to all communities. Through our innovative platform, we aim to:

  • Provide transparent, data-driven insights about reproductive health services
  • Combat misinformation through advanced fact-checking and AI-powered analysis
  • Empower advocates with comprehensive geospatial data and analytics
  • Build a collaborative community of researchers and advocates
